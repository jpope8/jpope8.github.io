Object-Oriented Techniques for IT Problem Solving introduced undergraduate students to developing solutions for business and information technology problems using object-oriented programming. Topics included object-oriented analysis and design, UML, classes, inheritance, polymorphism, exception handling, data modeling, testing, debugging, and implementation in Java.
