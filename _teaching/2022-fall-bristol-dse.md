Data Science Enrichment supported MSc students through applied enrichment sessions focused on building a machine-learning model from a public or partner dataset, demonstrating results in a JavaScript application, documentation writing, version control, project planning, and peer-to-peer presentations. James Pope led TensorFlow, model training/testing, LaTeX, and project documentation sessions.
