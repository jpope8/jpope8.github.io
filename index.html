Overview

About

Senior Lecturer in Data Science at the University of Bristol's School of Engineering Mathematics and Technology. With over three decades of engineering experience spanning academia, industry, and military service, my research sits at the intersection of machine learning, edge computing, and real-world sensing systems. I hold a Ph.D. in Information Technology from George Mason University and have authored 50 peer-reviewed works including 10 journal papers, 38 conference papers, and 2 book chapters.
